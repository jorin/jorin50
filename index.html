…a quick word about gifts…

I'm running out of time on this, so if you send me something I might not get it done by the 15th! Feel free, but also, sorry, I may give it to you later!

I worry about how much junk I have (which I love, so I'm not about to get rid of it, but if I don't get more, I won't develop that relationship to new things and keep accumulating).

I also appreciate how exchanging things can cement the memory and be sort of fun, so to honor that but ALSO not deal with receiving gifts, I'd like to be the one that hands the items out ... but like I said, I'm not getting rid of my stuff.
